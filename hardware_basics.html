Hardware Basics

What is hardware?
Hardware is defined as the collection of physical parts that helps in building a system.

Components of hardware:
1. Core components
-- Monitor
-- Hard Disk/Hard drive
-- Keyboard
-- Mouse/Touch pad/Trackball
2. Multimedia components
-- DVD/CD drive
-- Video card
-- Sound card
-- Speakers
-- Headset/Headphone/Microphone
3. Other peripheral device components
-- Printer/Scanner
-- CD burner, CD recorder, CD-R (read), CD-RW (read/write)
-- Modem
-- USB flash drive
-- Webcam
-- Digital camera
-- Digital Voice Recorder
-- Camcorder
